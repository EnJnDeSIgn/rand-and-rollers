Ian's new knots: by Ian J Norris

I try to create time.
But the more I try to create it, the more it collapses.
And the more it collapses, the more urgently I try to make it.
And the more urgently I try, the less time I have.
So I chase the time I cannot catch,
and in chasing it, I outrun the very moments
that might have held me still.
