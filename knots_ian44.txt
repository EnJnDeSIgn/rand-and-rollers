Ian's new knots: by Ian J Norris

I seek brave spaces.
But bravery reveals what I fear.
And fearing what bravery reveals, I retreat.
And retreating, I call myself unbrave.
And calling myself unbrave, I seek brave spaces again
to prove I am not what I fear I am.
Thus I circle the threshold,
never stepping in, never stepping out,
forever proving and disproving my own courage.
