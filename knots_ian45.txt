Ian's new knots: by Ian J Norris

I want to be on time.
But time refuses to be on me.
And because time refuses to be on me,
I chase it as though it were a garment I forgot to put on.
And in chasing it, I fall further behind.
So I try to catch the time that won’t catch me,
and in trying, I miss the moment
that might have let us meet.
