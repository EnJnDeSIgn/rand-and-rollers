Ian's new knots: by Ian J Norris

I want inclusion.
But I stand in the doorway, unsure whether to enter or guard it.
And guarding it, I keep myself out.
And keeping myself out, I insist I was never invited.
And insisting I was never invited,
I guard the doorway even more fiercely.
Thus I bar the threshold
to protect myself from the exclusion
I am performing.
