Ian's new knots: by Ian J Norris

I need space to be myself.
But when I take space, I feel I am taking too much.
And when I feel I am taking too much, I give it back.
And when I give it back, I disappear.
And when I disappear, I long for space again.
So I seek the space that would let me be myself,
but the moment I enter it,
I give it away to prove I deserve it,
and in proving I deserve it,
I lose the self I needed the space to find.
