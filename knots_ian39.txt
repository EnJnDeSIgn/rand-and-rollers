Ian's new knots: by Ian J Norris

My style says:

push them down,
then go to the police.

Their style says:

go to the police,
without pushing them down.

I think my style is stronger,
because I push them down.
But when I reach the police,
I am guilty
because I pushed them down
before I got there.

When they reach the police,
they are innocent
because they did not
push them down
before they got there.

My strength makes me guilty.
Their uselessness
keeps them safe.
